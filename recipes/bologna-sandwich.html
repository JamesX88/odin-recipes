Bologna Sandwich Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a tasty Bologna Sandwich. Bologna Sandwich is good. We should all eat more Bologna Sandwich. It's easy to freeze, reheat, and therefore unlikely to spoil like most dishes.

If you have any questions about Bologna Sandwich recipes, please don't ask me because I am just making this up as I go so you'll need to google your own answers.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Bologna
  • American Cheese
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread
  2. Add mustard to one slice of bread
  3. Add slice of American Cheese
  4. Add 1 to 15 slices of Bologna to your sandwich
  5. Microwave for 15 seconds
  6. Enjoy.
