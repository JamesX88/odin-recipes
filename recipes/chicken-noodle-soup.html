Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a tasty Chicken Noodle Soup. Chicken Noodle Soup is good. We should all eat more Chicken Noodle Soup. It's easy to freeze, reheat, and therefore unlikely to spoil like most dishes.

If you have any questions about Chicken Noodle Soup recipes, please don't ask me because I am just making this up as I go so you'll need to google your own answers.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Broth
  • Chicken Breast
  • Noodles
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken.
  2. Cook your noodles
  3. Add your chicken broth to a pot and heat
  4. Add chicken breast to broth
  5. Add noodles to broth
  6. Add carrots
  7. Heat it up!
  8. Enjoy.
